Plan Wisely

Best Time to Visit

Timing is everything. Depending on the destination, you might encounter tropical monsoon seasons, intense heat waves, or perfect winter ski snows. Here's a quick reference checklist compiled by our travel guides:

Europe

Spring (May-June) & Autumn (Sept-Oct) offer optimal weather with fewer crowds.

Southeast Asia

Dry Season (November to February) is cool, pleasant, and perfect for beaches.

North America

Summer (June-August) for hiking, and December-March for snow resort areas.

Caribbean & Maldives

December to April represents prime sunny weather with minimal rainfall.
