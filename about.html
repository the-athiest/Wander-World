Our Origin

Designing Adventures Since 2011

WanderWorld Travels started with a simple belief: travel should be transformative, secure, and uniquely personal. Founded in Boston as a two-person regional guide company, we have expanded to become a leading global booking platform connecting travelers with verified hotels, guides, and customized tour packages across 120 countries.

Our local expertise and 24/7 client assistant networks ensure that from the moment you submit booking details to the day you arrive back home, your experience is entirely hands-free and unforgettable.

Our Mission

To connect travelers with local cultures and nature, providing extremely safe, custom, and professional tour packages that inspire discovery and protect local tourism ecosystems.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and sustainable travel planning platform, using digital tools to personalize journeys while offering zero hidden-fee bookings.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

From a two-person startup to a global travel leader — every year brought a defining chapter to our story.

2011
Agency Founded

Started as a small local tour guiding firm in Boston with just two employees, organizing weekend getaways and hiking guides.

2015
International Expansion

Opened offices in Rome and Tokyo, growing our tour package roster to 50+ international destinations across four continents.

2020
Going Fully Digital

Launched our all-in-one online booking portal with flexible reservation tools and AI-powered custom package design for every traveler.

2026
50,000+ Happy Clients

Celebrating a landmark milestone of serving 50k adventurers globally with an outstanding 4.9-star average satisfaction rating.
